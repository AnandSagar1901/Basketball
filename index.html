What is Basketball?

Basketball is essientally soccer except that you dribble with your hands and you score by making shots.

How do you play Basketball?

Each team has 12 players. 5 players play at a time. If a player fouls another player there is a penalty. This penalty may vary based on when and what the foul was. For example if someone fouls another player while they're shooting it is a shooting foul. The player who got fouled gets 2 freethrows, each one worth one point(A normal shot is 2 points and if it is outside the three point line it's three points) If a player does 5 or 6 fouls they are ejected from the game. The game is split into 4 quarters. Each quarter 12 minutes. So one game is 48 minutes. At the beginning of each game 1 player from each team goes to the half court line for the jump-ball. The ref throws the ball in the air and the objective of the players to knock the ball back towards his or her's teamates.
